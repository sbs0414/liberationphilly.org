Why Direct Action

Please join us in forging the path to animal liberation. Veganism is not enough, the animals need your voice. DxE is based off of past social justice movements. We turn lonely vegans into empowered activists, and together we can change the world for animals!

Internationally DxE has been featured in USA Today, The New York Times, CBC News, The Washington Post, Wall Street Journal, and more.

Systemic Change. Community Building. Social Science. Global Network. Nonviolent Direct Action. Open Rescue.
